# Requirements
- Regular participation
- Active participation in group activities during the class
- Short presentation on News Media Avoidance (30 - 40 minutes)

# Final Paper (Hausarbeit)
Submission Deadline: September 1st, 2026, at 12:00 (noon)
Submission Format: All papers must be submitted via Moodle.
Guidelines: Students must follow the official guidelines for final papers (Leitfaden für Seminararbeiten).
Topic Selection: The final paper should address a topic related to the course. Possible areas include: (1) Media usage theories and concepts; (2) News avoidance
Students are encouraged to schedule an appointment with the instructor to discuss their paper topic and structure. It is recommended to do so before July.
